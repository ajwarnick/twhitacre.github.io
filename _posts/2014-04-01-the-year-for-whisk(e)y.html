If you’ve followed me on twitter or instagram you know that I like to indulge once in a while. So far my drink of choice has been gin. Plymouth Gin to be exact. As great as gin is, I have been told I should be drinking whiskey, at least some of the time. This includes all forms, bourbon, scotch, irish, rye and the likes. I’ll be honest, so far my favorite is rye with scotch being a close second but I have a long list to try and will hopefully be adding to the list as time goes on.

Even my wife is pushing me towards it by getting me the book Drink More Whiskey as well as some nice low ball glasses, whiskey stones and everything else one might need.
