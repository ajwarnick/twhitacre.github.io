Quite often, I get asked what kind of set up I have and what programs I use on a daily basis. About a year ago, I wrote a post, but below is an updated list... this time I'll stick to development related programs.

Also, below you'll find some info my current hardware setup as well.

Software

  • Coda 2 - came out in 2012 and I've been very pleased with it.
  • Sublime Text 2 - even though Coda has been updated... I still turn to ST2 when I need nothing but pure speed.
  • MAMP Pro - running sites locally.
  • Terminal - I used to use iTerm... but switched back since I feel this does a good enough job on it's own. Also, I use Coda 2's terminal as well.
  • ColorSnapper - grabs colors
  • ForkLift - switched from Filezilla to ForkLift. Haven't looked back....
  • Chrome - even though it's a browser... it's one of the best tools a developer can have in his toolbox
  • Fireworks CS3
  • Photoshop CS6

 

Hardware

  • 2009 iMac - 16gb Ram, 500gb Hard Drive - 2 monitors connected to it.
  • 2012 Macbook Pro 15" Retina - 8gb Ram, 256gb Hard Drive
  • iPhone 5 - 16gb
  • Google Nexus 7 16gb (my only non apple device - everyone should have at least one!)
